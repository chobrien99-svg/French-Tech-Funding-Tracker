<p>Microsoft France has unveiled the 15 startups selected for the third cohort of its GenAI Studio.</p><p>Chosen from more than 180 applications, the startups were selected for their technological maturity and large-scale impact potential across sectors including health, education, finance, industry, and the environment. </p><p>Over four months, they will receive intensive, tailored support, including up to $350,000 in Azure credits, access to more than 11,000 AI models, expert mentoring, and a dedicated workspace at STATION F. </p><p>The program is designed to help startups industrialize their solutions and scale across Europe and beyond, with a strong focus on responsible and secure AI. This third edition is backed by a powerful ecosystem of partners including Mistral AI, NVIDIA, GitHub, Databricks, ENGIE, and STATION F. </p><p>With this initiative, Microsoft aims to support 2,500 French startups with AI by 2027 and foster the emergence of global technology champions.</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><img src="https://news.microsoft.com/source/emea/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/Microsoft-GenAI-Studio-Batch-3-1024x575.png" class="kg-image" alt="" loading="lazy" width="1024" height="575"></figure><p><strong>The startups are: </strong></p><p><strong>Marketing &amp;
