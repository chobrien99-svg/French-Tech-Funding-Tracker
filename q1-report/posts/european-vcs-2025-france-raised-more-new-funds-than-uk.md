<p>For much of 2025, the narrative around European venture capital fundraising was bleak. <a href="https://newsletter.dealroom.co/vc-fundraising-dire-straits?ecid=ACsprvsWTEeHI4AylD6Eh3ct5vC7NJB9_1lHf1cAteowM2cYW5GITKDOflJgsUh7dvmeEq9FAWsU&utm_campaign=Weekly%20newsletters&utm_medium=email&_hsenc=p2ANqtz-9RCnIh6n2oD-rk07Zb_Ow9bk4vWppO33Iip0E3kKZMK4wkDScq8tEiiLdJNLymi-pQoojxKF94ozRIqg2hOPQ7gyMSh6-wehRz0NUk0gqbvIe0B5k&_hsmi=0&utm_content=394463469&utm_source=hs_email" rel="noreferrer">But according to Dealroom</a>, European venture capitalists raised $20.5bn across 247 new funds in 2025. That’s down from the $27.1bn raised in 2024, but hardly a freefall.</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><img src="https://www.frenchtechjournal.com/content/images/2025/12/image-64.png" class="kg-image" alt="" loading="lazy" width="1120" height="787" srcset="https://www.frenchtechjournal.com/content/images/size/w600/2025/12/image-64.png 600w, https://www.frenchtechjournal.com/content/images/size/w1000/2025/12/image-64.png 1000w, https://www.frenchtechjournal.com/content/images/2025/12/image-64.png 1120w" sizes="(min-width: 720px) 720px"></figure><p>The headline story, however, is that for the first time in recent memory, France overtook the UK in VC fundraising.</p><p>French venture capital firms raised $5.1bn in new funds in 2025. UK VCs, by contrast, raised just $3.7bn, a 56% drop year-on-year. The result is a symbolic and structural shift: the UK accounted for just 18% of European VC fundraising in 2025, well below its historic 30–40% share, and even lower than during the Brexit vote year of 2016.</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><img src="https://www.frenchtechjournal.com/content/images/2025/12/image-62.png" class="kg-image" alt="" loading="lazy" width="1120" height="821" srcset="https://www.frenchtechjournal.com/content/images/size/w600/2025/12/image-62.png 600w, https://www.frenchtechjournal.com/content/images/size/w1000/2025/12/image-62.png 1000w, https://www.frenchtechjournal.com/content/images/2025/12/image-62.png 1120w" sizes="(min-width: 720px) 720px"></figure><p>For more than a decade, London has been Europe’s default venture capital hub, hoovering up capital, talent, and international LP attention.
