<H1><center>🧮 Data Point: In Your Debt</center></H1>
<!--kg-card-end: html-->
<p>Private debt is back in France, baby! Not to fuel the next wave of startups, but to quietly clean up the last one.</p><p>Private debt is the unsexy cousin of venture capital. Instead of buying equity, funds lend money to companies. In the French tech ecosystem, that usually kicks in <strong>post-Series B and beyond, </strong>when startups are no longer scrappy but well short of the mythical exit. Think growth financing, acquisition funding, or simply extending runway without diluting founders. Basically, a lifeline.</p><p><a href="https://www.franceinvest.eu/activite-des-fonds-de-dette-privee-en-france-en-2025/?ref=frenchtechjournal.com" rel="noreferrer"><strong>According to the latest study by France Invest and Deloitte</strong></a>, €15.9B was deployed across 379 deals, up 25% in value and 20% in deal volume compared to 2024. Meanwhile, €14.8B was raised in 2025, an increase of 74% from 2024.  Which means while VC funding may be mortally wounded and lies bleeding to death in the street while onlookers ignore the body and go about their day, there’s suddenly a lot more non-dilutive capital sloshing around.</p><p>Alas, poor Yorick, this isn’t fueling a new wave of hypergrowth. The main purpose is refinancing, accounting for 42% of invested amounts. In other words, companies (and their PE backers) are extending existing debt rather than rushing into new expansion bets.</p><p>For now, those lenders are hoping that this strategy will keep these startups alive and that when it comes to this debt, they'll turn out to be, well, like the Lannisters.</p><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><img src="https://media.tenor.com/LhTAL_m_UVEAAAAC/tyrion-lannister-peter-dinklage.gif" class="kg-image" alt="" loading="lazy" width="498" height="303"></figure><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card"><img src="https://www.frenchtechjournal.com/content/images/2026/03/dette-1.png" class="kg-image" alt="" loading="lazy" width="2000" height="1119" srcset="https://www.frenchtechjournal.com/content/images/size/w600/2026/03/dette-1.png 600w, https://www.frenchtechjournal.com/content/images/size/w1000/2026/03/dette-1.png 1000w, https://www.frenchtechjournal.com/content/images/size/w1600/2026/03/dette-1.png 1600w, https://www.frenchtechjournal.com/content/images/2026/03/dette-1.png 2291w" sizes="(min-width: 720px) 720px"></figure><div class="kg-card kg-button-card kg-align-center"><a href="#/portal/signup" class="kg-btn kg-btn-accent">Become a paid subscriber</a></div><hr><div class="kg-card kg-cta-card kg-cta-bg-purple kg-cta-immersive  kg-cta-has-img  " data-layout="immersive">
